Conference Overview

The Second Annual Conference 2026 is the official international flagship event of IJBTCS, bringing together researchers, developers, and industry leaders.

Hosted in Egypt by Bioinformatics Gate, this edition emphasizes the integration of Biotechnology with Computational Science, promoting interdisciplinary research that bridges experimental lab innovation with AI.
