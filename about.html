About IJBTCS 2026

Bridging the Gap Between Biotechnology and Computational Science.

Vision & Mission

The 2nd Annual International Conference on Advances in Biotechnology and Computational Science (IJBTCS 2026) aims to provide a premier interdisciplinary platform for researchers, practitioners, and educators to present and discuss the most recent innovations, trends, and concerns in the fields of Biotechnology and Computational Science.

Our mission is to foster collaboration between laboratory research and AI-driven modeling, accelerating experimental innovation through computational power.
