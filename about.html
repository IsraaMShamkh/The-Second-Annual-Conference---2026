About the Journal

The International Journal of Biotechnology and Computational Science (IJBTCS) is a leading open-access academic journal based in the UK. We specialize in bridging the gap between laboratory innovation and advanced computational modeling.

  • Peer-Reviewed Academic Excellence
  • Fast-Track Publication Options
  • Global Research Impact

Vision & Mission

Vision: Global leadership in Biotechnology by creating an integrated research environment that supports innovation and academic excellence.

Mission: Bridging the gap between lab innovation and computational modeling, providing an international lighthouse for researchers to solve complex challenges.

Frequency Quarterly
First Decision 3-7 Days
Peer Review Within 30 Days

Journal Scope

Biotechnology Section

Molecular biology, microbiology, and genomics interpreted via computational methods.

Computational Science

Machine learning, deep learning, and theoretical modeling for life sciences.

Tools & Pipelines

New computational tools and platforms designed to accelerate biological discovery.
